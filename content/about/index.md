      text: |
       Established in 1992, the [Research Institute](https://www.recercasantpau.cat/en/) at Sant Pau Campus is a leading biomedical hub dedicated to advancing health through cutting-edge science. Affiliated with the Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB) since 2009 and integrated into the [CERCA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CERCA_Institute) system of Catalan research centers since 2011, <span style="color: #d32f2f; font-weight: 600;">IR SANT PAU</span> fosters a collaborative ecosystem where researchers conduct groundbreaking translational work spanning basic, epidemiological, and clinical science to applications.

       The campus hosts the historic [Hospital de Sant Pau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hospital_de_Sant_Pau) – a six-century-old medical landmark, the Cochrane Iberoamerican Centre, Primary Care centers, Barcelona Public Health Agency, UAB, among others to transform discoveries into patient care.

       At the intersection of tradition and innovation, IR SANT PAU trains international scientists while delivering tangible healthcare breakthroughs, embodying its mission to bridge knowledge and wellbeing and it is recognized as one of Catalonia’s most productive research institutions.
